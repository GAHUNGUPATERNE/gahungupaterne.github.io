About

I am a Research fellow at the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences, specializing in spatial epidemiology and public health data analysis. My work focuses on understanding disease patterns through geospatial modeling and data-driven public health interventions.

Research Interests

  • Spatial and Spatio-Temporal Modeling
  • Disease Mapping and Risk Analysis
  • Bayesian Statistics and INLA
  • Health Disparities and Environmental Epidemiology
